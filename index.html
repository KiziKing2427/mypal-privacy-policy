Privacy Policy for MyPal

Last Updated: June 2, 2026

MyPal ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, process, and protect your information when you use the MyPal mobile application.

MyPal is operated from Thailand and is committed to complying with applicable privacy laws, including the Thai Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (2019) ("PDPA").

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create and use an account, we may collect:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (stored securely)
  • Profile information you voluntarily provide

Chat and Communication Data

To provide messaging, translation, and language-learning features, MyPal may collect and store:

  • Chat messages
  • Conversation history
  • Language preferences
  • AI conversation interactions
  • Translation requests and responses

Usage Information

We may collect information regarding your use of the application, including:

  • App activity
  • Feature usage
  • Device information
  • Diagnostic and performance information
  • Error and crash reports

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Enable messaging and communication features
  • Provide real-time translation services powered by OpenAI
  • Deliver AI-powered conversations and language-learning assistance powered by OpenAI
  • Improve application functionality and performance
  • Maintain platform security and prevent abuse
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

AI and Translation Services

MyPal uses OpenAI services to provide:

  • AI-powered conversations
  • Language-learning assistance
  • Real-time message translation
  • Language detection

When you send messages using AI-powered features or translation features, the content may be processed by OpenAI to generate responses, translations, and language-related functionality.

For more information about OpenAI's privacy practices, please visit: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy/

Data Storage

User account information, chat messages, and related application data are stored using Firebase services provided by Google.

Firebase is used for authentication, database storage, and application infrastructure.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect stored information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Cross-Border Data Transfer

To provide AI-powered conversations, language-learning features, and translation services, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Thailand, including the United States, where our service providers operate.

Service Providers That May Process Data

Service Provider Purpose Data Processing Location
OpenAI AI conversations, language learning, language detection, and translation United States
Firebase (Google) Authentication, database storage, and infrastructure United States and other regions operated by Google

By using MyPal, you acknowledge that your information may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions with data protection laws that may differ from those in Thailand.

Your Rights Under the Thai PDPA

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to:

  1. Access your personal data
  2. Request correction of inaccurate data
  3. Request deletion of your personal data
  4. Restrict certain processing activities
  5. Receive your data in a portable format
  6. Object to certain processing activities
  7. Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below.

How to Delete Your Account and Data

You can delete your account and associated personal data in two ways:

Option 1: Within the App

Go to Settings → Account → Delete My Account

Option 2: By Email

Send a deletion request to mypal.support@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account

We will process deletion requests within 30 days. Some information may be retained for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes as permitted by law.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to:
