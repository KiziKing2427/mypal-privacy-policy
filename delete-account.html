MyPal Account Deletion Request

Users may request deletion of their MyPal account and associated personal data by emailing:

mypal.support@gmail.com

Please send the request from the email address associated with your MyPal account.

We will process eligible deletion requests within 30 days.

Data Deleted

  • Account information
  • Username / ChatID
  • Profile information
  • Chat history associated with the account

Some information may be retained where required by law, security, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution purposes.

Contact: mypal.support@gmail.com
